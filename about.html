About Sunny Cafe

Our story and passion

A Warm Welcome

Sunny Cafe was founded in 2020 by coffee enthusiasts who dreamed of creating a welcoming space where people could start their day with a smile. Nestled in the heart of the city, our cafe bathes in natural light, offering a sunny escape from the hustle and bustle.

We source our beans from sustainable farms and bake fresh pastries every morning. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and a cozy atmosphere.
